Our programmes

Guidance workshops

Our Workshops help refugees find the university pathways best suited to them, set goals, and work on achieving them.

Peer-to-peer learning

Peer-to-peer learning programmes are a platform to share advice, support, and stories about accessing university.

English classes

We emphasise dialogue and draw on student experiences, making content relevant to refugees’ lives. Our classes don’t rely on expensive course materials.
